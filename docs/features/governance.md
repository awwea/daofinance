# Governance

By participating in the DAO project, which deals with the allocation of liquidity and leasing of trading software, one acquires the right to share in the profits. DAO supports democratic, decentralized finance, all experts agree that the decentralized finance that will be the future trillion market.

* DAO membership for an unlimited time, with the purchase of DAO tokens
* DAO token value increase through the DAO activities in DEFI
* Authentic participation in the development of the DAO through democratic principle.

Traditionally in DAO, you get dividends on the organization's investments. These are automatically paid to the members and investors via smart contract. DAO Token has a price of USD 1,000 when the DAO goes live. Dividends approx. 100% p.a. The number of issued DAO tokens is limited to 25000 pieces. The target price of the DAO token in 5 years is USD 100000.
