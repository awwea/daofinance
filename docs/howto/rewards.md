# Rewards

#### **What is a Unilevel MLM Plan?** <a href="#id-6f67" id="id-6f67"></a>

A Unilevel MLM plan is defined as a compensation plan that has only one business level and all sponsored distributors/members are placed directly in the first tier. This plan has no downsides and the distributor’s recruitment efforts will directly benefit him/her. It is a universal circuit and can be found in all compensation circuits.

Generally, the distributor sponsorship tree in any compensation plan is represented by a single-level plan. So, this plan is present in all MLM compensation plans and that is why it is called the “Universal Plan”.
