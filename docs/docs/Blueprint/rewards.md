# Rewards

#### **What is a Unilevel Plan?** <a href="#id-6f67" id="id-6f67"></a>

A Unilevel plan is defined as a compensation plan that has only one business level and all sponsored distributors/members are placed directly in the first tier. This plan has no downsides and the distributor’s recruitment efforts will directly benefit him/her. It is a universal circuit and can be found in all compensation circuits.

Generally, the distributor sponsorship tree in any compensation plan is represented by a single-level plan. So, this plan is present in all MLM compensation plans and that is why it is called the “Universal Plan”.

#### **Compensation in Unilevel Plan** <a href="#id-5b9e" id="id-5b9e"></a>

-   There are no complexities involved in the structure of a unilevel compensation plan.
-   The compensation earned can touch the sky if the distributors are good enough.
-   You can earn some really good bonuses in addition to the higher residual income.
-   The plan works well as far as all network marketing business models are concerned.

Unilevel bonus and compensations are based on individual performance and group efforts. Let’s see how popular compensations look like in a unilevel plan.

**Direct Bonus**\
The distributor generates a sales volume of $ 1000 and has already reached through own effort the Level 2 with 7% direct bonus. He gets paid by the company with $ 70.\
\
**Level Difference Bonus**\
The distributor has a team worker in the Level 1 with 5%, who generates a sales volume of $ 1000.\
\- team worker gets Direct Bonus $ 50\
\- distributor gets paid $20
