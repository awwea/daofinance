# FIAT Transfers

What is fiat P2P?

DFI P2P is more than just a platform for depositing and withdrawing cryptocurrencies, it's also a key service for streamlining global fund transfers. DFI P2P enables the conversion of Bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies into cash, allowing users to select their preferred payment method, currency, and price.&#x20;

By leveraging the internal crypto transfer function and P2P cash-out features, international fund transfers are convenient, cheap, and secure.
